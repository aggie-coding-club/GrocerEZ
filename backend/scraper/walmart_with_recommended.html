      About This Item

      We aim to show you accurate product information. Manufacturers, suppliers and others provide what you see here, and we have not verified it.

      Bring home the classic taste of Red Delicious Apples. These apples have an enticingly sweet flavor with white flesh and low acidity. With higher antioxidants due to their deep red skin, these crisp and juicy apples are a satisfying afternoon snack. You can also use them in a variety of recipes. For breakfast, use these apples to make a rich and creamy yogurt parfait or a nutritious juice blend. Slice these apples and use them to add flavor to a lunchtime salad or spread peanut butter on them for a protein-filled snack. Red Delicious Apples are used in many tasty desserts like apple cobbler, apple crisp, and apple pie. Get creative in the kitchen and make homemade apple butter or applesauce. However you choose to use them, Red Delicious Apples add sweetness to any meal.
